# Tiramisu Cake Recipe

## Ingredients

**For the cake:**
- 6 eggs, separated
- 150g sugar
- 200g mascarpone cheese
- 300ml heavy cream
- 2 tbsp cocoa powder
- 24-30 ladyfinger biscuits
- 300ml strong espresso, cooled
- 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. **Prepare layers:** Dip ladyfingers briefly in cooled espresso and layer in a 9x13" dish.

2. **Make cream:** Whip egg whites until stiff peaks form. In another bowl, beat yolks with sugar until pale.

3. **Combine:** Fold mascarpone and vanilla into yolks, then fold in whipped cream and egg whites.

4. **Assemble:** Spread half the cream mixture over first ladyfinger layer. Add another layer of dipped ladyfingers, then remaining cream.

5. **Finish:** Dust generously with cocoa powder. Refrigerate 4+ hours or overnight.

## Serves
8-10 people

## Notes
- Use pasteurized eggs if concerned about raw egg consumption
- Can substitute mascarpone with cream cheese for a tangier flavor
