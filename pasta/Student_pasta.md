# Student pasta


## Ingredients
- Pasta 250g
- Water 0.5 l
- Olive oil 3Ts
- Tuna 1 tin
- Salt 0.5 ts

Optional:
- Fresh tomatoes
- Pepper

## Instructions

In pot:
- Add wather
- Add pasta
- Boil for 15 minutes

In pan:
- Pour in olive oil
- Add boiled pasta
- Add Tuna
- Fry for 10 minuntes
- Add otpional ingredients

Enjoy your meal!
