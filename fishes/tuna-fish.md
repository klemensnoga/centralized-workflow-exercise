# Tuna fish

---

## Ingredients

---

- 1 tuna steak
- 2tbsp olive oil
- sald and cracked blask pepper
- 1 lemon wedge

## Instructions

---

- Pat the tuna steak dry with paper towels and season both sides with salt and pepper.
- Heat the olive oil in a skillet over high heat until it begins to shimmer.
- Place the tuna in the pan and sear for 1.5 to 2 minutes per side for a medium-rare center.
- Remove from the pan, let it rest for a minute, and serve with a squeeze of lemon.
